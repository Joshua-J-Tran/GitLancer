Designed for stealth infiltration, espionage, and silent assassination, the unit serves as a living ghost—capable of bypassing both conventional and biometric security infrastructures. Unlike the ASM-class (Asmodeus line), which focuses on brute-force combat, the SCYLLA blueprint prioritizes precision, silence, and adaptability.
